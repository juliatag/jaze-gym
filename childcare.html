Child Care Center

Our affordable mom-tailored plan gives you access the Child Care Center and Children Classes to help you stay healthy even if you have a busy mom life.

How does it work?

When you sign up, choose the Mom plan. After, you will be abale to reserve a timeslot (30min-2h) for your children before you bring them to the Child Care Center. Reservations can be done in person or by phone for up to a total time of 6h and up to 2 weeks in advance.

Why we provide onsite childcare?

We know that it could be hard to keep up with your helath and fitness goals while striving to be the perfect mom. As a women only gym, we find it imperative to support you in your journey in a convenient, affordable and acessigble way.

Work-life balance and the importance of self-care

While the Child Care Center can give you the peace of mind that you need, our classes tailored to moms and children and moms to be can give you the extra tools and support you need to achieve a well-balanced lifestyle.
