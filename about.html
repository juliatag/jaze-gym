Mission

Our mission at Jaze Gym is to support all women on their health journey. Our committment to you is steadfast which is why we offer 24/7 services. We pride ourselves on our ability to assist you in reaching your goals, while we take care of the rest. At our gym, women feel empowered and encouraged to be their healthiest selves!

Convenient underground parking!

Our location at 420-B Baker Street is right near the Mont Royal metro for those using transit and has free underground parking for those driving cars.


Opening hours

We're excited to announce that we are open 24/7! We understand the importance of time we want to be available at your convenience. We also offer virtual classes as part of our programming which are accessible to you from the comfort of your own home.

A brief history

We started as a small space, owned and operated by women for women. We knew that a fitness center that catered to women's comfort and family values was missing in this lovely neighbourhood. We quickly realized that the need for this kind of service was greater than we realized and so we moved to a bigger space with a gorgeous view and a stunning pool!

Our Founder

Jane Smith is the brains and beauty behind the creation of this amazing gym. She is a caring mother of 6 children and has been able to maintain her fitness while taking care of her family. She wanted to ensure that all women, like you, had the freedom to do the same as her by providing you with the gym and child care to facilitate your needs.
